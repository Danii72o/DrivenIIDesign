About Me

I'm Danielle, creator of Driven II Design, a passionate web developer enthusiast with a solid grounding in computer science. Graduating with a degree in computer science has equipped me with the knowledge to delve deep into the intricacies of the digital world. My journey into the realm of technology started with an insatiable curiosity that blossomed into a true passion for the endless possibilities it offers. Armed with a robust understanding of computer science principles, I embark on every project not just as a coder but as a creator, striving to shape memorable web experiences.
